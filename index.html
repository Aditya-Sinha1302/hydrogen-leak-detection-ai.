Strategic Overview

Introduction

Hydrogen holds immense potential as a clean energy carrier, offering zero-emission fuel for various applications. As industries shift towards sustainability, safe hydrogen storage and usage become paramount.

Problem Definition

Hydrogen is highly flammable and leak-prone due to its small molecular size. With a wide flammability range (4-75%), undetected leaks present critical ignition and explosion hazards.

Project Objectives

Develop an AI-driven, real-time monitoring system utilizing TCN models and sensor arrays to predict, detect, and mitigate hydrogen leakage risks before critical danger thresholds.
